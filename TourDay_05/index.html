Arriving in Toulouse

Toulouse, known as “La Ville Rose” for its terracotta brick architecture, has long been a center of culture and innovation. Once a powerful medieval city-state, it later became pivotal in the aerospace industry, home to Airbus headquarters. The Basilica of Saint-Sernin anchors its Romanesque legacy, while lively squares reflect a youthful student population. Toulouse balances deep Occitan heritage with modern dynamism, making it one of France’s most distinctive and economically important regional capitals.
